VocaDB mobile app version.

Available features
• Searchable for songs, artists, albums and events
• Highlighted songs, new released albums and events
• Song ranking
• Watch any song PV that contains Youtube url.
• Save your favorite songs, artists or albums (For temporary)

VocaDB is a free to use database for discography information about Vocaloid and related voice synthesizers.
For more information please visit at  http://vocadb.net

Also this app is open source, For any bugs report, suggestion or feedback is welcome.

Github: https://github.com/VocaDB/VocaDB-App
